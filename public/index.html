About Me

My name is Jessica Berrios, I am a current graduate student at the University of New Haven studying Cybersecurity & Networks. I also received my Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity & Networks in May of 2023 from the University of New Haven.

Currently, aside from attending my classes, I am working on research involving the forensic analysis of ransomware written in Go.

In addition to my research, I enjoy attending extracurricular activities such as the university hacking club and the Women in Cybersecurity Student Chapter. Outside of my academics, other hobbies include reading and doing CTFs.
